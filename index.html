About SecureWrist

Our project merges luxury watchmaking with technology by linking each luxury watch to an NFT from its manufacturing stage, ensuring indisputable authenticity through the blockchain. A discreet NFC chip in the bracelet provides a direct connection to the NFT via a simple scan, creating an unparalleled experience that blends tradition and innovation for discerning enthusiasts.
